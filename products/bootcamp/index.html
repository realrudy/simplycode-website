Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

Students of all ages and backgrounds can apply. No prior coding experience is necessary.

What will I need?

A computer with internet access and a modern web browser. All tools used will be free.

How does the free hosting work?

Upon completion of the bootcamp, you'll receive a free domain name and 1-year hosting package for your website project.

What if I miss a session?

All sessions will be recorded and made available to enrolled students. Our mentors also provide additional support.
