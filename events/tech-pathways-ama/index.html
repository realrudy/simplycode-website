Tech Pathways AMA Workshop

March 9th, 2025
5:00 PM - 7:00 PM PST
Online (Zoom)

Event Overview

Our Tech Pathways Ask-Me-Anything Workshop was designed to help students discover various career paths in technology and understand how to prepare for them. We invited industry professionals to share their experiences, insights, and advice on navigating the diverse and dynamic tech industry.

Expert Panelists

Professionals from various tech fields shared their career journeys and answered student questions

Career Insights

Participants gained practical advice on education requirements, skills needed, and daily responsibilities in tech roles

Interactive Q&A

Students had the opportunity to ask specific questions and receive personalized guidance
